About the Game

I call this game simplified Blackjack because:

  • It’s a one-player game only (you vs. the dealer).
  • You can’t split, double down, or buy insurance.
  • It plays with a single deck.
  • Dealer “stands” at 17. (The computer cannot draw another card if its total is 17 or greater)

Play the game.
